ADMINISTRATIVE SUPPORT

Frequently Asked Questions

Understand the official compliance and registration guidelines below.

How do we secure funding for student organization events? +

Submit a digital budget proposal sheet inside your Organizer dashboard. Upon review and sign-off from your designated faculty board advisor, the system automatically routes the proposal to the Student Affairs desk for final dean stamp and release.

What is the turnaround time for Dean approval signatures? +

Digital signatures inside CampusHive are completed within 24 to 48 hours. The system has built-in notification alerts that ping deans and administrative offices about pending approvals directly on their portal desks.

How does the digital QR ticket check-in audit log work? +

When students register for events, a digital QR badge is generated. At the door, coordinators scan the code using any standard smartphone camera. The attendance roster is updated in real-time on your dashboard ledger.
