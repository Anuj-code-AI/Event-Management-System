Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event? expand_more
Creating an event is simple. Navigate to your dashboard, click "Create Event," and follow our guided wizard to add details, upload promotional assets, and set up your ticketing tiers in minutes.
Can we track ticket sales? expand_more
Yes, CampusHive provides real-time sales tracking and financial reporting. You can monitor conversion rates, ticket availability, and student discount applications through our live analytics dashboard.
Is there student support? expand_more
We offer 24/7 technical support for both administrators and student attendees. Whether it's a lost ticket or a setup question, our dedicated campus success team is always a click away.
